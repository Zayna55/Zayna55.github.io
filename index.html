Guyana

Guyana is South American country, with a more Carribean culture. The 2017 population estimate is 773,303, which is a small number of people for a country that is 214,970km squared, compared to New Zealand which has a similar area and an estimated nearly five million people.

Guyana used to be called British Guyana, but became just, Guyana in 1966 when it gained independence from Britain.
